🇿🇲 Zambia Digital Commerce Rights

ECT Act No. 21 of 2009 · CCPA No. 24 of 2010

⏱️ 7-Day Cooling-Off Period

Under Section 36 of the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act No. 21 of 2009, you have the right to cancel any digital transaction within 7 business days of receiving goods — without giving any reason and without penalty. A full refund must be issued within 30 days of cancellation.

📦 30-Day Delivery Guarantee

Under Section 37 of the ECT Act, if an item is not delivered within 30 days from the date of purchase, the agreement is automatically cancelled and you are entitled to a full refund. Pasampo's biker delivery operates within 2–4 hours in Lusaka; long-distance orders must ship within 7 business days.

🚫 "No Refund" Clauses Are Illegal

Under Section 49 of the Competition and Consumer Protection Act No. 24 of 2010 (CCPA), any clause in a digital contract that says "No Refunds", "All Sales Final", or "No Returns" is void and unenforceable under Zambian law. Sellers on Pasampo are legally prohibited from including such clauses.

📱 Mobile Money Reversal

If a transaction fails, is cancelled within the cooling-off period, or qualifies for a refund, Pasampo will initiate a Mobile Money reversal within 72 hours to your original Airtel Money or MTN Mobile Money number. You will receive an SMS confirmation when the reversal is processed.

⚖️ Dispute Resolution

Disputes must be raised with Pasampo Support within 7 days of the transaction. Unresolved disputes may be referred to the Zambia Competition and Consumer Protection Commission (ZCCPC) at zccpc.org.zm or +260 211 227 999. Pasampo complies fully with ZCCPC enforcement directives.
